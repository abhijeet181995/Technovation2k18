The object of the bot should be to follow a black line on a white background without losing the black track and navigating several orthogonal turns and curves autonomously. The bot has to complete the course in minimum time starting from starting point.

Event comprises of two rounds. Qualifiers of round one will compete in round two. Arena for round one will be uploaded soon.

1. Course time :time will be measured as bot crosses the starting line till it crosses the finish line.

2. The bot is deemed to crossed line when forward wheel or forward leg of bot has crossed over the line.

3. Every team will be given two rounds and best two will be entertained.

4. Maximum of five minutes will be given for every chance.

5. A bot that wander off the arena will be disqualified.

6. If bot loses its path it will regain its path where it lost or at an earlier path ( that already traversed).

7. More than ten minutes of delay of team will not be entertained. The team will be disqualified.


1. Robot dimensions:20cm x 20cm x 15cm.

2. Arena specifications 120cm wide 220cm long.

3. Weight of bot under 5kg.

4. Bot should autonomously controlled.

5. Only power supply through battery will be accepted.

6. Maximum 4 members are allowed in one team.

7. And each team should have their separate one bot.

8. Arduino or AVR microcontrollers are allowed.
